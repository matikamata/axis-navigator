Saṅkhāra

volitional formations
Volitional formations conditioned by ignorance — the second link in dependent origination. Kamma-generating mental intentions that produce future rebirth. In the tilakkhaṇa context: sabbe saṅkhārā aniccā / dukkhā — all conditioned things are impermanent and unsatisfactory.
